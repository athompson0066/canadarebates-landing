About CanadaRebates

CanadaRebates helps homeowners discover and claim government rebates for home improvements — from heat pumps to insulation to windows.

Our Mission

Billions in Canadian government rebates go unclaimed every year. Most homeowners don't know they qualify, and the process to find rebates is scattered across 13+ different government lists.

We consolidate everything into one searchable platform, then connect you with verified contractors who help you claim what's yours.

How We Work

  • Aggregation: We merge federal, provincial, and utility rebate programs into one database
  • Verification: Contractors are verified against official government contractor lists
  • Matching: Homeowners get matched with verified contractors in their area

For Contractors

  • Add your website and contact info
  • Get featured placement in your city
  • Receive leads from homeowners ready to hire
